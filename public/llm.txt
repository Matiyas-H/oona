## How It Works

Traditional voice AI pipelines lose context at every handoff:
1. Audio → STT (Speech-to-Text)
2. Text → LLM (Language Model)
3. Response → TTS (Text-to-Speech)
Result: 500-1000ms typical first response

Omnia Voice approach:
1. Audio → Embeddings
2. Direct to reasoning
Result: ~250ms time to first response
